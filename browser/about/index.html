About Me

My name is Karam. I'm an ambitious software engineering student hailing from Amman, Jordan.
I'm currently studying for my Bachelor's degree at the Budapest University of Technology and Economics (BME). My interests in the field of computer science are mostly related to the applications of data science and machine learning.
In my free time, I play football, lift weights, and enjoy reading philosophy.

I studied for 12 years in the National Orthodox School. From the 9th grade till graduation, I studied the British system, IGCSE O-Levels and A-Levels (IAL). For my A-Levels. I studied Physics, Mathematics, Arabic, and Chemistry (AS-level only). Funnily enough, I did not study ICT or Computer Science in high school.

Fun Fact: I studied Civil Engineering for a year before switching to Computer Science Engineering, yet I studied both in Budapest University of Technology and Economics (BME), which was founded in 1782. The university was the first institute in Europe to train engineers at university level, and according to Wikipedia, it is considered the world's oldest institute of technology which has university rank and structure.
